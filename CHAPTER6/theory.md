# BASIC MATH AND STATS WITH SQL

***Baic Math Operator***

| Operator   | Descrpition    |
|:---------------: | :---------------: |
|`+`| Addition   |
|`-`| Substraction   |
|`*`| Mutiplication   |
|`/`| Division (returns the quotient only, no remainder)   |
|`%`| Modulo (returns just the remainder)   |
|`^`| Exponentiation   |
|`straight /'`| Square root   |
|`straight straight /`| Cube root   |
|`!`| Factorial   |
